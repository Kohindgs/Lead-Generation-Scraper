## 2. LinkedIn Strategy

### 2.1 Profile Optimisation (Do This First!)
Before outreaching, optimise the sender's LinkedIn profile:
- **Headline**: "Helping [Industry] Businesses Generate More Leads Online | Digital Marketing"
- **Banner**: DGenius Solutions branded banner with website URL
- **About**: Lead with the value you provide (not your CV)
- **Featured**: Case studies, client results, testimonials
- **Creator Mode**: ON — post 3x/week to build authority

### 2.2 Content Strategy (Warm up your network)
Post content BEFORE sending cold outreach:
- **Monday**: Industry stat or insight (e.g., "97% of consumers research online before buying")
- **Wednesday**: Mini case study ("How we helped a dental clinic get 40 new patients in 30 days")
- **Friday**: Educational tip ("3 SEO mistakes killing your Google ranking")

Post consistently for 4 weeks before aggressive outreach.

### 2.3 Connection Request Campaign
**Daily limit**: 20 connection requests max (to avoid LinkedIn restrictions)

**Sequence**:
1. Send connection request (personalised, ≤300 chars)
2. Wait 2–3 days
3. Send welcome DM on connection
4. Wait 3–5 days
5. Send value-add message with case study
6. Wait 5–7 days
7. Soft pitch / call CTA

### 2.4 LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Recommended)
Use Sales Navigator for:
- Advanced filters (company headcount, seniority level, posted on LinkedIn)
- InMail credits (60/month) for unreachable profiles
- Lead lists + CRM sync
- Saved searches with alerts for new matches

**Search filters to use**:
- Seniority: C-Suite, Director, VP, Owner
- Function: Marketing, Business Development, Operations
- Industry: (per Tier 1 list above)
- Company size: 1–200 employees
- Geography: Target regions
- "Changed jobs in last 90 days" → Buying signal (new decision-makers)
- "Posted on LinkedIn in last 30 days" → Active, engaged leads

### 2.5 LinkedIn Content Ads (Paid)
For faster results alongside organic:
- **Lead Gen Forms**: Collect emails without leaving LinkedIn
- **Sponsored InMail**: Reach anyone's inbox (0.4%–0.8% conversion)
- **Document Ads**: "Free Digital Marketing Audit" as lead magnet
- **Budget**: Start with $20-30/day; optimize weekly

**Target Audience for Ads**:
- Job title: Marketing Director, CEO, Business Owner
- Industry: Real Estate, Legal, Healthcare
- Company size: 11-200
- Location: Top metro areas

## 3. Google Strategy

### 3.1 Google Maps (Local Business Targeting)
**Best for**: Service businesses with physical presence
**Method**: Search categories near target locations, score by:
- Review count < 50 → High priority (need reputation management)
- No website → High priority (need web design + SEO)
- Rating < 4.0 → High priority (need review management)
- No Google Business Profile optimisation

**Outreach approach**: Phone call or email to business owner/manager directly.
**Pitch**: "Free Google Presence Audit" — low commitment, high value.

### 3.2 Google Search (Intent-Based Targeting)
**Search queries that reveal hot leads**:
- `"digital marketing agency" "looking for" site:linkedin.com`
- `"need more leads" OR "struggling with marketing" site:reddit.com`
- `"hiring digital marketing manager" site:linkedin.com`
  *(Companies that tried DIY and are now ready for an agency)*
- `"marketing budget" "2025" "b2b" site:linkedin.com`

### 3.3 Google Ads for Lead Capture (Inbound)
Run Google Ads targeting businesses searching for marketing help:

**Keywords**:
- "digital marketing agency for real estate"
- "SEO services for law firms"
- "Google Ads management small business"
- "social media marketing agency"
- "how to get more leads for my business"

**Landing Page Offer**: "Free 30-Minute Digital Marketing Audit"
**Conversion Rate Target**: 3–5% landing page → lead

## 4. Outreach Messaging Framework

### 4.1 The "AIDA-P" Framework for Cold Outreach
- **A**ttention: Open with something specific about THEIR business
- **I**nterest: Identify a pain point we spotted
- **D**esire: Show the outcome/result (with proof)
- **A**ction: One clear CTA
- **P**ersonalisation: Use their name, company, industry, specific data

### 4.2 LinkedIn Connection Request Formula
```
Hi [First Name], I help [Industry] businesses [specific outcome].
Noticed [specific observation about their company].
Would love to connect — I may have some ideas for [company].
```
**Example**:
> Hi Sarah, I help dental clinics in NYC fill 20+ appointment slots per month using Google.
> Noticed Smile Bright has 8 Google reviews — there's a big opportunity here.
> Would love to connect!

### 4.3 Cold Email Subject Line Formulas
- `Quick question about [Company]'s Google ranking`
- `[Industry] businesses in [City] are missing this`
- `[Specific pain point] — here's what we'd do`
- `[First Name], found something about [Company]`
- `How [Similar Company] got 3x more leads in 60 days`

### 4.4 Follow-Up Cadence
| Day | Action | Goal |
|-----|--------|------|
| Day 1 | Send cold email / connection request | Awareness |
| Day 3 | Follow-up #1: Add social proof | Re-engage |
| Day 7 | Follow-up #2: Value-add (quick tip / audit) | Provide value |
| Day 14 | Follow-up #3: Breakup email | Last attempt |
| Day 30 | Add to nurture email sequence | Long-term |

## 5. Lead Scoring System

Leads are scored 0-100 based on:

| Factor | Max Points |
|--------|-----------|
| Tier 1 industry | 25 |
| C-Suite / Owner seniority | 20 |
| Email found & verified | 8 |
| LinkedIn profile | 4 |
| Phone available | 3 |
| Pain points detected (×5 each) | 20 |
| Website audit issues found | 12 |
| Buying signals (×2 each) | 5 |
| Google Maps signals | 3 |

**Score interpretation**:
- 75–100: 🔴 Hot lead — prioritise immediately
- 55–74: 🟡 Warm lead — outreach within 48 hours
- 30–54: 🟢 Cold lead — add to nurture sequence
- 0–29: ⚪ Not qualified — skip or archive
